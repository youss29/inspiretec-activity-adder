How to add these to the shared calendar

1. Open the shared workbook, Activity Calendar sheet.
2. Click Copy rows for Excel above (or click inside the box and press Ctrl and C).
3. In the sheet, click the first empty cell in the leftmost Week column, just under the last activity.
4. Paste with Ctrl and V. Week, Theme, Channel and Status colours all apply automatically.
5. Click the Date column arrow and choose Sort Oldest to Newest so the new rows drop into the right week.
Dates are written year, month, day so Excel reads them correctly. The Theme is filled from the fortnight theme for that date, you can edit it before adding. One row is one activity, a repeat creates one row per date.span>
